This doc aims to help transitioning from Twitter to Mastodon.

Follow all peeps on Mastodon which you are following on Twitter

1.) Drag and drop this link to your browser's bookmarks-bar:

Add export-link

2.) Go to bridge.joinmastodon.org and activate app-permissions for your Twitter and your Mastodon-account.

3.) Click the bookmark and scroll to the bottom of the page, there should be a link now, saying: "Download all matching Mastodon-users as CSV-file". Click it, save the file.

4.) Go to your Mastodon-account, click "settings" and then "import". For "Import type" choose "Following list", click "Browse" and upload the exported file.

This is a copy of https://github.com/ida/skriptz/blob/master/js/browser-injects/bookmarklets/mastodon.html, for questions and comments please add an issue in https://github.com/ida/skriptz.
